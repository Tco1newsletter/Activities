Guru Nanak Jayanti

Nursery to Grade 2

GURU NANAK JAYANTI VIDEO

"Selfless Service and Sacrifice resonates the essence of being human" To inculcate the feeling of humanity and mankind and to spread the message of oneness and purity, TCO1 to celebrate Guru Nanak Jayanti on 7th November in the circle time period. The celebration commenced on a revered and serene note with morning prayers and meditation. The thought of the day, further apprised everyone on the importance of serving humanity for a cause. The informative session highlighted the crucial role played by Shri Guru Nanak Singhji in the liberation of humanity. The teacher address further highlighted to be sensitive towards the needs of fellow beings and that how it can help create a harmonious and better world. The prime objective of this day was to teach children the learnings of Guru Nanak dev ji and ensuring they learn to lend a helping hand always no matter their caste, creed or age. Students also sang shabads in the Class and showed their devotion to Guruji. Children learnt the 3 main principles to follow in life - Kirat Karo (Honest Living), Naam Japo (Meditate on God), Vand Chakho (Share with needy).

Diwali Celebration

Nursery to Grade 2

“To illuminate darkness, you must believe in the presence of light”. Diwali is the festival of lights. We at TCO1 believe in nurturing our students in every possible manner. They are the shining stars of our future world just like the diwali lamps removing the darkness of night and bringing glory. To sensitize our students to be aware of their traditional background and understand the mythological reasons behind the festival we did a diya painting activity to encourage them to go local. Students were also guided to celebrate ‘Eco - Friendly Diwali’ and ‘Go Green”.
